GoHighLevel is a comprehensive marketing automation platform that integrates CRM, marketing automation, sales funnels, email & SMS campaigns, appointment scheduling, and analytics into one powerful platform. - **All-in-One Solution**: CRM, funnels, emails, SMS, websites, and automations in one place
- **White Label Software**: Brand the platform as your own and resell it
- **Low Startup Costs**: Replace multiple subscriptions (Mailchimp, Calendly, Podium, etc.)

## Quick Start Action Plan

### Week 1: Foundation
1. Sign up for GoHighLevel ($97 Starter or $297 Unlimited)
2. Complete the platform training
3. Set up your agency account and branding
4. Create your service packages and pricing

### Week 2: Build Your Offer
1. Create a demo sub-account to show prospects
2. Build 2-3 template funnels for your target niche
3. Set up automation templates you can clone
4. Create your sales presentation

### Week 3: Get Clients
1. Identify 20 local businesses in your target niche
2. Reach out via phone, email, or in-person visits
3. Offer a free audit of their current marketing
4. Present your solution and packages

### Week 4+: Deliver & Scale
1. Onboard your first clients
2. Document your processes
3. Create SOPs for repeatability
4. Consider hiring help as you grow
